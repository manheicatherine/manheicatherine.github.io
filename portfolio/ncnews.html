Introduction

Northcoders News is my fullstack project of social news aggregation, web content rating, and discussion website. It is a social news and discussion website where you can stay up-to-date with the latest news and content. The platform features a user-friendly interface and allows you to rate and discuss various topics.

Features of the application:

- Login to NC News - View the articles (all articles or by topics)
- Sort the article by date, comments or votes etc
- Click a single article
- Leave comments on article
- Delete comments on article
- Vote for articles
